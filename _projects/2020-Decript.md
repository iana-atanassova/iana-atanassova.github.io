Project for developing tools to detect representations of personal data in texts.

**Duration:** January 2020 - June 2022

Objective: Develop Natural Language Processing tools to ensure GDPR compliance (General Data Protection Regulation).

Funding: Interreg France-Switzerland 2014-2020 and Communauté du Savoir, Switzerland. FEDER funding: 307K €.

**Academic partners:** HEG (Switzerland), HEG Arc (Switzerland)

**Private partners:** ERDIL (France), Global Data Excellence (Switzerland)
