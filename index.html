Funded Projects

Current projects

PI of project InSciM - "Modelling Uncertainty in Science" (2021 - 2025), funded by French ANR JCJC.

Supervisor of project on “Automatic correction of text responses to exams” with start-up e-Cole, funded by Technopole de la Réunion, with the objective of technology transfer to the start-up

Supervisor of the project EMONTAL - "Extraction and Ontology Modeling of Subjects and Places for the Exploitation of the Documentary Funds of Bourgogne Franche-Comté" - PhD thesis (2020-2024), funded by Bourgogne Franche-Comté Region.

Participant in project TheoScit (2020-2023), funded by French ANR.

Participant in project SEO-ELP (2019-), funded by Fr-Educ, Franche-Comté, France.

Past projects

PI of the project DecRIPT - "Detection of the Representations of Personal Data in Texts" (2019-2022), funded by Interreg France-Switzerland 2014-2020 and Communauté du Savoir

Participant in project WEBSO+ (2016-2018, funded by Interreg France-Switzerland): development of a platform for strategic technological and competitive intelligence and e-reputation integrating semantic processing, text classification and sentiment analysis

Participant in project SARS (2015-2017, funded by Franche-Comté, France): tools for scientific writing using lexical databases and Information Retrieval

As Post-Doctoral Fellow at Concordia University, Montreal, Canada (2014):

  • Design and implementation of processing chains for the digitizing, OCR and semantic annotation of journal articles. Collaboration with BANQ (Bibliothèque et Archives nationales du Québec) and Érudit. Semantic analysis of journal articles with applications to social and historical studies, in collaboration with Prof. Jean-Philippe Warren.
  • Studying the impact of the IMRaD (Introduction, Methods, Results and Discussion) structure of scientific papers on the distribution of citations. Citation context analysis, in collaboration with Marc Bertin, Vincent Larivière and Yves Gingras. Applications to visualization and science maps.

As associate researcher at STIH, Paris-Sorbonne University, Paris, France (2012 - 2013): research in the field of semantic annotation of scientific articles, Information Retrieval, Information Extraction and Interfaces.

Current teaching activities

Course marager of MSc in Natural Language Processing (speciatly in MSc Languages and Foreign Cultures LLCER) at Université de Franche-Comté, since 2017.

Teaching in MSc in Natural Language Processing: Methodology in NLP, Computer Programming for NLP, Information extraction and IR, Cognitive Methods and ML, Software engineering for NLP.

Teaching in BSc Languages and Foreign Cultures, specialty Natural Language Processing, Université de Franche-Comté: Introduction to computer programming for NLP.
